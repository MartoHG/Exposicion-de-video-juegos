04 — PAST SIMPLE · Before AI
No Adaptation,
No Personality
▶ SPEAKER 2
Game worlds were also much smaller and more limited. Developers had to design every single level manually. There was no adaptation to the player — if you were bad at the game, it didn't care. It was always the same experience for everyone.
In Space Invaders, aliens just moved left and right — the same pattern, every single time.
